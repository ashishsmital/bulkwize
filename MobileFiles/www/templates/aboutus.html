About Us

We are a live B2B ecommerce platform aimed at retailers, wholesalers & institutional buyers.

In todays competition where more and more buying is moving towards online ecommerce, businesses are under tremendous pressure to offer competitive prices. The price you buy at, plays a major role in how you can competitively price your end products. A large part of business buying is still bounded by geographical boundaries, finding the appropriate brands/suppliers, commission to various distributors and middlemen etc resulting in higher procurement costs.

We aim to solve this problem through our unique B2B ecommerce platform where you can:-

  • Search for widest range of products for your business right on your mobile phone
  • Find products across widest price spectrum to suit every business need
  • Order online and get doorstep delivery.

Products on our platform are very competitively priced because we deal directly with brands & manufacturers. Due to our global operations we are able to provide you higher volume based discounts. To start with will also be offering cash on delivery of up to Rs. 25000.
