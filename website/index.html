About Us

    What documents are required for registration?

    We will mainly need a Photo Id, a registered business proof and an undertaking that confirms that the products purchased here will be used for further trading and is not for end-user consumption.

    • Any of the following documents accepted as photo id proof:
      • Aadhar card
      • Driving license
      • Passport
      • Voter Id card
    • Any of the following documents are accepted as registered business proof:
      • Valid business license issued by Central or State Government
      • Copy of outward sales invoice or company letterhead to be submitted along with business license
      • Authorization letter in case primary member is not a license holder
    • Validity of membership
      • Membership is valid till the expiry of the business license provided
      • For licenses that do not have defined validity, membership is valid for 5 years from the date of registration
      • Post expiry of the membership, a member can’t purchase from the store, unless the renewed valid license document is submitted and expiry extended on the system (member needs to submit a copy of recent outward invoice for renewal, in case of licenses with no defined validity)
      • Membership is terminated in case any of the submitted documents is found invalid
    • Download the undertaking form here
