About Us

In todays competition where more and more buying is moving towards online ecommerce, businesses are under tremendous pressure to offer competitive prices. The price you buy at, plays a major role in how you can competitively price your end products. A large part of business buying is still bounded by geographical boundaries, finding the appropriate brands/suppliers, commission to various distributors and middlemen etc. We aim to solve this problem through our unique platform, which for the 1st time allows businesses to buy stuff in large volumes online. Products on our platform are very competitively priced because we deal directly with brands & manufacturers. Due to our global operations we are able to provide you volume based discounts. To start with we also offer cash on delivery of up to Rs. 25000.

I am a supplier

Our platform helps manufacturer or a supplier (brand owner) increase the outreach of their products. By choosing to offer your brands/products via our mobile platform your product gains a global outreach across boundaries of location and distributor. Your business will no longer suffer just because you do not have a huge distribution n/w. In today’s digital commerce world where a brand’s best connect with their customer is the mobile, our mobile presence ensures that your product is only a click away from your consumer. If you wish to offer your products via our platform, please fill out the adjacent details and we will get in touch with you.
